About This Personal Project

Plannies Mate helps the developers behind making planning data more accessible to everyone in Australia.

This suite of tools helps me (Ian) and hopefully you as a developer quickly find which scrapers need attention, diagnose issues with existing scrapers, and develop new ones when council websites change.

Note: Pages derived from private repos require login access. Contact Ian if you are on the planningalerts-scrapers team and don't have access.
